#### IF YOU ARE USING ETHERNET, DO THE FOLLOWING:

**TURNING OFF WIFI & BLUETOOTH \[ REQUIRED ]**

**ONLY DO THIS STEP IF YOU HAVE WIFI/BLUETOOTH AVAILABLE ON YOUR MOTHERBOARD, OTHERWISE YOU CAN SKIP IT.**

<details>

<summary>Why is this "required"? (PLEASE READ)</summary>

For an Ethernet connection, we can permanently spoof your MAC address. However, this isn't possible on a WiFi connection (wireless).

If you are only able to use WiFi:

1. Try to acquire Ethernet (much higher chance of unban)
2. Use the "WiFi Mac" option in the "Special" tab of the spoofer (later on, after spoofing your normal serials), then disable LAN from BIOS and Bluetooth from BIOS as well.

</details>

> **ASUS:** ADVANCED > ONBOARD DEVICES CONFIGS > WIFI (WLAN) & BLUETOOTH **\[DISABLED]**

> **MSI:** ADVANCED > INTEGRATED PERIPHERALS > WIFI (WLAN) & BLUETOOTH CONTROLLER **OR** ONBOARD CNVi MODULE CONTROL **\[DISABLED]**

> **GIGABYTE:** ADVANCED/PERIPHERALS > WIFI (WLAN) & BLUETOOTH **\[DISABLED] (NOTE: MOST GIGABYTE MOTHERBOARDS DO NOT GIVE YOU THIS OPTION. YOU WILL NEED TO FULLY TAKE OUT YOUR WIFI CARD FROM THE M.2 OR PCIE SLOT,** [**HERE IS A USEFUL VIDEO**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9j83rixFHxw) **TO GUIDE YOU THROUGH IT.)**

> **ASROCK:** ADVANCED > CHIPSET CONFIGURATION > WIFI (WLAN) & BLUETOOTH **OR** ONBOARD WAN DEVICE **OR** WAN RADIO/BT On **\[DISABLED]**

**If none of these options are available in your BIOS, please mention this in the ticket along with a picture of the related page in BIOS.**
